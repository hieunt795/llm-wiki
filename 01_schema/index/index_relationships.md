- [[Accountability_Independence_Trade_off]] — **Thesis:** The relationship between independence and accountability is characterized by a negative de jure correlation but a positive de facto complementarity; accountability serves as the democratic prerequisite that legitimizes central bank autonomy.
- [[APAC_Private_Credit_Landscape]] — **Thesis:** Thị trường [[Private_Credit]] tại khu vực Châu Á - Thái Bình Dương (APAC) đang trải qua giai đoạn chuyển mình mạnh mẽ, được thúc đẩy bởi nhu cầu vốn bespoke và sự rút lui chiến lược của các ngân hàng do áp lực [[Regulatory_Implementation_Framework]].
- [[Asset_Encumbrance_And_Overcollateralization]] — **Thesis:** The perceived absolute safety of 'Covered Bonds' rests explicitly mathematically on massive 'Overcollateralization (OC)'; however, pledging massive excess volumes of high-quality assets strictly exclusively to covered bondholders triggers severe 'Asset Encumbrance', systematically hollowing out the residual balance sheet and dangerously explicitly subordinating standard aggregate depositors and senior unsecured creditors.
- [[Breakeven_Inflation_Metrics]] — **Thesis:** Breakeven inflation is the rate of inflation that equalizes the internal rate of return of an inflation-linked bond and a nominal bond of identical maturity, serving as a market-based measure of inflation expectations plus a technical risk premium.
- [[Central_Bank_Political_Economy_Dynamics]] — **Thesis:** The relationship between academia and central banking is a dynamic feedback loop where theoretical constructs provide foundations for policy, while practical crises force innovative breakthroughs that subsequently drive new academic research.
- [[Credibility_Growth_Trade_off]] — **Thesis:** The speed of disinflation (Cold Turkey vs. Gradualism) is determined by a central bank's credibility: high credibility allows for aggressive disinflation with minimal output loss, while low credibility necessitates a gradual approach to avoid a catastrophic sacrifice ratio.
- [[EMEA_Private_Credit_Landscape]] — **Thesis:** Khu vực EMEA (đặc biệt là các quốc gia GCC) đang chứng kiến sự dịch chuyển mạnh mẽ từ mô hình tài trợ qua ngân hàng (Bank-centric) sang sự kết hợp với [[Private_Credit]]. - [[Spot_Forward_Relationship]] — **Thesis:** Forward bond price = spot clean price + net carry. Net carry = borrowing cost - coupon income earned during holding period. Positive carry (coupon > repo rate) lowers forward price. Forward-spot basis narrows to zero by delivery date.
- [[Spot_Swap_Relationship]] — **Thesis:** Swap value = value of fixed-coupon bond minus value of floating-rate note. Fixed cash flows discounted at zero-coupon rates. Swap fairly priced when both legs have equal present value. Forward-starting swaps priced by removing near-term cash flows and solving for new fixed rate.
