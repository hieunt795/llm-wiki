## Thesis

Despite their supreme gravity in global macroeconomics, there is no monolithic consensus on what exact function structurally transforms a bank into a "Central Bank." The definition splits roughly between two academic frameworks: the Bindseil definition (liability-focused) and the Goodhart definition (asset-focused). Modern practice, as emphasized by Perry Warjiyo, has converged on a **Dual Mandate**: the absolute requirement to maintain both domestic currency value (price stability) and the integrity of the financial plumbing (financial stability). [[During_Fixed_Income_Ch05.md#page=35]] [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P2.md#page=4]]

## Definition Frameworks

### 1. The Bindseil Framework (Bank of Issue)
Bindseil defines a central bank through its unique liability characteristics:
- It issues abstract financial money of **ultimate quality** (cannot default in domestic nominal terms).
- It operates under a **public charter** for public policy objectives rather than private profit. [[During_Fixed_Income_Ch05.md#page=35]]

### 2. The Goodhart Framework (Lender of Last Resort)
Goodhart defines a central bank by its systemic role as the **Lender of Last Resort (LOLR)**.
- *Mechanism:* Fractional reserve banking (creating [[Inside_Vs_Outside_Money|Inside Money]]) is prone to runs. The system demands an apex entity capable of injecting unconstrained liquidity to solvent but illiquid institutions.
- *Evolution:* This definition acknowledges that entities like the Bank of England evolved organically into this role to prevent the collapse of London's financial infrastructure. [[During_Fixed_Income_Ch05.md#page=36]]

## The Modern Dual Mandate

Hậu khủng hoảng tài chính toàn cầu (GFC), ranh giới nhiệm vụ của NHTW đã mở rộng đáng kể. Perry Warjiyo bóc tách cấu trúc này:
- **Price Stability:** Kiểm soát lạm phát và tỷ giá hối đoái. Đây là tiền đề cho tăng trưởng kinh tế bền vững.
- **Financial System Stability:** Chuyển dịch từ vai trò LoLR thụ động sang giám sát **Macroprudential** chủ động (ví dụ: quản lý tỷ lệ LTV, down-payments).
- **Payment System:** Đảm bảo sự tin cậy, hiệu quả và an ninh của việc chuyển tiền, bù trừ và quyết toán, bao gồm cả việc thích ứng với các làn sóng FinTech. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P2.md#page=4-5]]

## Boundaries / Conditions

### The Ownership Complexity
Central bank ownership is diverse:
- **Nationalized:** e.g., Bank of England.
- **Member-Bank Owned:** e.g., US Federal Reserve Banks (technically private).
- **Publicly Traded:** e.g., Central Bank of Greece.
Regardless of ownership, the apex credibility requires **Operational Independence** from the fiscal state to prevent catastrophic [[Monetary_Financing_Mechanism|Monetary Financing]]. [[During_Fixed_Income_Ch05.md#page=34]]
