## Thesis
Cash represents the "Outside Money" of the highest order in a fiat system, providing a final settlement medium that does not rely on bank credit.

## Definition
Cash is distinguished by its operational properties:
1. **Finality:** Settlement is immediate upon physical transfer.
2. **Anonymity:** No record of the transacting parties is required.
3. **Decentralization:** No third-party intermediary (bank) is needed for the transaction.
