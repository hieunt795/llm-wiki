## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] Par curve = YTM values for bonds with same credit risk but different maturities, typically from recently issued bonds near par (price ≈ 100). Simplest observable curve. YTM limitation: assumes reinvestment at original YTM. Zero-coupon and forward curves derived from par curve via bootstrapping.
