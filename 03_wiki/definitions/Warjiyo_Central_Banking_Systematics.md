## Thesis

The book is organized to reflect the **Dynamic Feed-back Loop** between crises, theories, and policy responses. It prioritizes the "Policy Mix" paradigm as the terminal state of modern central banking evolution.

## The Five-Part Structure

1. **Part I: General Review (Ch 1-2):** Evolution, reforms, and the lessons of history (Gold Standard to post-GFC).
2. **Part II: Theoretical Review (Ch 3-5):** The role of money/credit, exchange rates in an open economy, and transmission mechanisms (Money View vs Credit View).
3. **Part III: Policy Framework (Ch 6-9):** Strategic and operational monetary policy (Monetary targeting, ITF, FIT).
4. **Part IV: Institutional Governance (Ch 10-12):** Building credibility, independence, accountability, and the new role of transparency/communication.
5. **Part V: The New Paradigm (Ch 13-15):** The **Central Bank Policy Mix**. Detailed modeling (IIF, DSGE) and the pioneered practices at Bank Indonesia since 2010.

## Methodological Goal
To advance the understanding of policymakers and researchers by presenting:
- Philosophical reviews.
- Quantitative analyses.
- Practical case studies (Indonesia as a primary example).
