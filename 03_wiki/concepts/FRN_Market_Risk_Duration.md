## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] FRNs are credit instruments, not interest-rate sensitive. Max duration = LIBOR reset frequency (3 months typical). As reset date approaches, duration declines. On reset date, FRN trades at par because investor receives current market LIBOR rate—no interest-rate disadvantage. Price movements driven by credit quality changes (issuer risk), not rates.
