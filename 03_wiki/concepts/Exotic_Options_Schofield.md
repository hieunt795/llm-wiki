## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] Exotic options defined by non-vanilla payoff at expiry. Barrier options: trigger activates (knock-in) or deactivates (knock-out) position on spot breach. 16 permutations (standard/reverse × knock-in/out × call/put). Binary/digital options: fixed payout if ITM, cheaper than vanilla. Path-dependent variants (one-touch, no-touch) cost 2× European due to increased exercise probability.
