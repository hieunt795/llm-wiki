## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] Relative value = "optimal/efficient way to express market view" (not merely cheap-rich). Two perspectives: (1) **Macro:** market's interest rate expectations, liquidity preference, preferred habitat, market segmentation. (2) **Micro:** identify underpriced vs overpriced assets, execute paired trade. Example: sovereign default risk view via asset swap spreads (directional + relative value). RV ≠ true arbitrage (which is rare, exploited immediately); RV accepts basis/execution risk. Chapter 5 applies RV framework to: (1) yield curve mechanics + formation theories, (2) spread measures (I-spread, Z-spread, OAS, TED), (3) practical sovereign bond valuation techniques.
