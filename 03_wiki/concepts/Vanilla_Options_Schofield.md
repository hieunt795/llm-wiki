## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] An option is a contract granting the holder the right but not obligation to buy (call) or sell (put) an underlying asset at a pre-agreed strike rate. Exercise rights depend on style: European (expiry only), American (anytime), or Bermudan (scheduled dates). Moneyness (ITM/OTM/ATM) determines exercise logic. Buyer's max loss = premium; seller faces unlimited loss on calls.
