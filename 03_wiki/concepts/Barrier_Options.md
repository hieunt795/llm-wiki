## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] Barrier option has trigger that either activates (knock-in) or terminates (knock-out) position on spot breach. Standard = barrier OTM; reverse = barrier ITM. Creates 16 permutations. Primary use: cost reduction. Barrier option always cheaper than European equivalent because position survives OR gets terminated early, reducing probability of payoff.
