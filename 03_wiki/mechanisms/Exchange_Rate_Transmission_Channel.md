## Thesis

For small open economies, the exchange rate channel is often the most potent MPTM. Perry Warjiyo bóc tách ranh giới của sự lan tỏa: sự thay đổi lãi suất không chỉ dừng lại ở biên giới quốc gia mà còn kích hoạt các dòng vốn xuyên biên giới, làm thay đổi tỷ giá và từ đó tái cấu trúc lại toàn bộ sức cạnh tranh ngoại thương cũng như áp lực vật giá trong nước. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=127]]

## 1. Transmission to Net Exports (The Indirect Channel)

Based on the **Interest Rate Parity**, a reduction in the policy rate ($i \downarrow$) makes domestic assets less attractive, leading to capital outflows and currency depreciation ($s \uparrow$).

### The Mechanism
- **The Logic:** Depreciation makes domestic exports cheaper and imports more expensive in real terms.
- **The Variable:** Net exports ($x$) respond based on the **Terms of Trade (ToT)**:
$$x_t = \alpha + \beta \tau_t + \gamma(y_t - y^*_t)$$
- **$\tau_t$ (ToT):** $\Delta p - (\Delta s + \Delta p^*)$.
- **Outcome:** If the [[Marshall_Lerner_Condition|Marshall-Lerner]] condition is met, net exports rise, stimulating economic growth ($y$). [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=127]]

---

## 2. Transmission to Inflation (The Direct Channel)

The exchange rate affects domestic prices through the **Pass-Through** effect (ERPT).

### The Mechanism
- **The Direct Hit:** Depreciation ($s \uparrow$) immediately raises the domestic price of imported finished goods, raw materials, and capital equipment.
- **The Expectations Hit:** Significant FX volatility can unanchor inflation expectations, leading firms to hike prices even for non-traded goods to protect margins.
- **The PPP Anchor:** $\Delta p_t = \alpha \Delta s_t + \beta \Delta p^*_t$. 
- **Outcome:** Domestic inflation ($\pi$) rises, often with a shorter lag than the interest rate channel. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=128]]

## Strategic Implications: EME Sensitivity

Perry Warjiyo highlights that this channel is critical for EMEs:
- **Small Open Economy Standard:** EMEs are often "price takers" in global markets. FX volatility is transmitted faster to CPI than in large advanced economies.
- **Dual Policy Role:** Because the exchange rate affects both the trade balance (growth) and inflation (price stability), central banks often use **FX Intervention** as a secondary instrument to "smooth" this transmission channel when it becomes too volatile. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=127]]

### Boundary Condition: Fiscal Risk Divergence
[WIKI] Cơ chế truyền dẫn từ lãi suất sang tỷ giá (IRP) giả định sự ổn định về mặt tài khóa. Khi rủi ro tài khóa đạt ngưỡng cực đoan, tương quan thuận giữa lợi suất và tỷ giá có thể đảo chiều thành tương quan nghịch (Yield tăng, FX sập).
