## Summary

[RAW-BOOK:5] RV not just "cheap/rich vs. others" but "which instrument best expresses my view?" Given view on EUR credit, can buy AAA sovereign OR buy bond future OR use option OR receive fixed IRS. Each has different risk/return profile. Framework: spot-forward-swap-option as 4 tools for trade design. Arbitrage (same asset, two prices) rare; RV exploits relative mispricing.
