## Thesis

The expectations channel is the most "psychological" MPTM. Perry Warjiyo bóc tách ranh giới của sự lan tỏa: chính sách tiền tệ không chỉ là những con số lãi suất thực tế, mà là một **Trò chơi niềm tin (Credibility Game)**. Nếu NHTW có uy tín cao, chỉ một tuyên bố về mục tiêu lạm phát cũng có thể thay đổi hành vi của hàng triệu thực thể kinh tế trước khi bất kỳ một đồng tiền nào được bơm ra. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=128]]

## 1. The Logic of Inflation Expectations

Inflation expectations are unobservable but drive real-world pricing. Perry uses the **Fisher Identity** to link them to market variables:
$$r^e_{t+j} = i^e_{t+j} + \pi^e_{t+j}$$
- **$r^e$:** Nominal interest rate expectations.
- **$i^e$:** Real interest rate expectations.
- **$\pi^e$:** Inflation expectations.

### The Estimation (Mishkin, 1990)
$$\pi^e_{t+j} = r^f_{t+j} - i^e_{t+j}$$
Central banks monitor the **Forward Rate** ($r^f$) as a proxy for what the market believes future inflation will be. If the forward rate rises, it signals that the market "expects" the central bank to fail its target, forcing a pre-emptive policy response. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=128-129]]

## 2. Transmission Mechanisms

### A. To Aggregate Demand (The Real Rate Path)
- **The Logic:** If agents expect high inflation, the **Expected Real Interest Rate** falls ($i^e = r - \pi^e$). 
- **Outcome:** This encourages current consumption and investment, effectively amplifying an expansionary stance.

### B. To Aggregate Supply (The Pricing Path)
- **The Logic:** Firms set prices and workers negotiate wages based on $\pi^e$. 
- **The Anchor:** If expectations are "anchored" to the central bank's target (e.g., 3%), firms will keep price hikes small even during temporary shocks. If unanchored, they will hike prices "just in case," creating a self-fulfilling prophecy. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=129]]

## Strategic Implications: The Credibility Anchor

Perry Warjiyo highlights that for EMEs, this channel is the foundation of the **Inflation Targeting Framework (ITF)**:
- **Consistency:** Credibility is earned through consistent achievement of targets.
- **Communication:** Transparent communication (e.g., Monetary Policy Reports) is a direct tool to manage the expectations channel.
- **Outcome:** Anchored expectations reduce the **Sacrifice Ratio** (the output loss needed to lower inflation). [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=129]]
