## Thesis

Since late 2023, under Governor Pan Gongsheng, the PBOC has shifted toward a more conservative FX management style. The framework effectively functions as a "crawling peg" to the US Dollar, where the daily fixing slope is the primary policy parameter, and market forces are frequently offset by the Counter-Cyclical Factor (CCF) to prevent pro-cyclical herd behavior.

---

## 1. The Daily Fixing Anchor

The daily fixing (midpoint rate) is the central pillar of RMB management. Spot prices are allowed to trade within a ±2% band around the fixing.
- **The "Piecewise Constant" Function:** [RAW-CLIP: Zhennan Li (2026-02-11)] PBOC often keeps the fixing nearly flat or moving along a linear slope, regardless of broad dollar volatility, to signal stability.
- **The Fixing Formula:** `Fixing(t) = Spot Deviation(t-1) + Overnight USD Move + CCF`.

---

## 2. Counter-Cyclical Factor (CCF)

The CCF is a discretionary adjustment used by the PBOC to "correct" market-driven pressure.
- **Appreciation Mitigation:** When the RMB faces strong appreciation pressure (e.g., strong trade surplus or weak USD), the PBOC uses a positive CCF to keep the fixing weaker than market forces suggest.
- **Depreciation Mitigation:** Conversely, during capital outflows, the CCF is used to keep the fixing stronger than the market.
- **Institutional Goal:** To break the "herd effect" and encourage counter-cyclical behavior among market participants (exporters/importers).

## 4. Interaction with Domestic Interest Rate Reform (2024-2025)

[RAW-ACADEMIC: Guo (2025)] Sự cải cách khung vận hành tiền tệ nội địa có tác động trực tiếp đến quản trị FX:
- **Interest Rate Transmission:** Việc tách rời LPR khỏi MLF và liên kết với **7-day Reverse Repo Rate** giúp đồng nhất mỏ neo chính sách nội địa. Điều này cung cấp một tín hiệu lãi suất rõ ràng hơn cho các luồng vốn (capital flows), giúp PBOC quản lý chênh lệch lãi suất (spread) với USD hiệu quả hơn.
- **Liquidity Control:** PBoC sử dụng hành lang lãi suất (SLF - Ceiling / Excess Reserve - Floor) để kiểm soát DR007, từ đó ổn định chi phí vốn nội tệ và giảm thiểu các biến động đầu cơ trên thị trường FX Swap.

## 5. Intervention Dynamics (2026 Case Study)

[RAW-CLIP: Zhennan Li (2026-05-07)] In early 2026, the PBOC executed its largest interventions in recent years:
- **March 2026:** Sold an estimated **$64 billion** USD to mitigate RMB depreciation during the Iran-Israel conflict/oil shock.
- **April 2026:** Bought an estimated **$51 billion** USD (accumulating reserves) to slow the RMB's appreciation pace as tensions eased and fundamental factors (BBOP) drove the currency higher.

---

## 4. The Correlation Shift (CFETS vs. USDCNY)

A notable consequence of the conservative "crawling peg" approach since late 2023 is the shift in correlation between the CFETS RMB Index (the basket) and the USDCNY spot rate.
- **Pre-2023:** Negative correlation (typical of a flexible regime).
- **Post-2023:** **Positive correlation**—the weaker the RMB against the USD, the stronger it typically becomes against the basket (and vice versa).
- **Macro Implication:** This approach stabilizes the USDCNY rate at the cost of less flexibility in the real effective exchange rate (REER), potentially weakening the "automatic stabilizer" role of the currency for trade.

---

## 5. Fundamental Drivers vs. Valuation

- **The BBOP Model:** [RAW-CLIP: Zhennan Li (2026-02-10)] RMB moves are best explained by the **Basic Balance of Payments (BBOP)**—Current Account + Direct Investment + Portfolio Investment—and the broad US Dollar index.
- **Valuation Mirage:** IMF REER valuation is a poor indicator for short-term (1-year) FX forecasting. Current account surpluses may reach record highs ($700B in 2025), but capital account outflows (portfolio investment by residents) often offset these gains.
