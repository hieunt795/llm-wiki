# [[Evolution_ITF_Indonesia]]

## Thesis
[WIKI] The first decade of ITF implementation in Indonesia demonstrated that institutional discipline and transparent communication can successfully anchor expectations, even when supply-side structural shocks cause inflation to deviate from the target corridor.

## Performance Evaluation
[RAW] ITF implementation has recorded a number of successes in the form of substantive improvements to economic fundamentals... (1) maturation of institutional existence; (2) policy signal clarity; and (3) increased policy credibility. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=275]]

[RAW] Observations using surveys and empirical testing showed a previous or ongoing shift in the formation of public inflation expectations from backward looking to forward looking, which has had a positive impact on reducing the degree of inflation persistence. [[Perry_Central_Bank_Policy_P3.md#page=275]]

[LLM] Despite these successes, structural issues on the supply side—including fuel price hikes (2005, 2008, 2013, 2014) and volatile food prices—have frequently pushed inflation beyond target boundaries, highlighting the limitations of monetary policy alone in controlling Headline CPI.
