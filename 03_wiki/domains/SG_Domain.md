# DOMAIN: Singapore (SG)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$600 billion (mostly used for investment, not funding consumption).
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~160% (Gross), but Net Debt is negative (Net Creditor).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Diversified, SGS (Singapore Government Securities).
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: Aiming for balance over the political cycle.
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Surplus/Balance.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Low (Debt is used to build market infrastructure, not to spend).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+15% to +20% (Massive surplus).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Massive goods and services surplus.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Global hub for multinational headquarters.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Global wealth management hub.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$350 - $400 billion.
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~6-7 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: High nominal (hub status), but strong net position.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: MAS manages the official foreign reserves.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Policy Framework: Exchange rate-based (S$NEER), not interest rate-based.
    - [LLM] Interest Rate: Follows global USD rates (SORA).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: SORA (Singapore Overnight Rate Average).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): MAS Standing Facility.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Net Exports: Main driver.
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~35%
    - [LLM] Investment: ~25%
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~70% (Financial, Trade, Tourism).
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~25% (Electronics, Biomedicals).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~0%
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~1.8% - 2.0% (Extremely low).
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: ~3% - 4% YoY.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~16% - 17%
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~1.0%
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~80%
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~120%
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: High (Family offices, Hedge funds).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$600 billion (SGX).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$150 billion (SGS).
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Singapore Exchange (SGX).
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): CDP (Central Depository).

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: High (MAS).
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: Highest (AAA rating).
- **Rule of Law:**
    - [LLM] Regulatory Efficiency: Highest.
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: ASEAN, CPTPP.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): AAA / Aaa / AAA.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)

- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/365.
