# DOMAIN: Japan (JP)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$9 trillion
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~260% (World's highest).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Long-dated, mostly domestic-owned.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-5% to -6%.
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit, targeting balance by late 2020s.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Low due to suppressed yields, but extremely sensitive to rate hikes.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+3% to +4% (Structural surplus, driven by primary income).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Volatile, often in deficit due to energy imports.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Relatively low compared to other G7 nations.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Large outflows as Japanese investors seek yield overseas.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$1.2 trillion (Second largest globally).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~15 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: Net creditor nation status (World's largest).

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$5 trillion (Exceeds GDP).
    - [LLM] QE/QT Status: Transitioning from QE/YCC to "normalized" policy in 2024.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Short-term rate (~0% to 0.1%).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Negative (Inflation ~2-3% vs policy rate ~0%).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: TONAR (Tokyo Overnight Average Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): BoJ JGB purchases, Loan Support Program.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~55%
    - [LLM] Investment: ~25%
    - [LLM] Government Spending: ~20%
    - [LLM] Net Exports: Contribution varies with JPY strength.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~70%
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~29% (Automotive, Robotics).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~1%
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~2.5% (Structurally low).
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: Shunto negotiations are critical (targeting 4-5% in 2024-2025).

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~14%
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~1.0% (Very low).
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~60% (High liquidity surplus).
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~180%
    - [LLM] Corporate vs Household Leverage: High corporate cash reserves, moderate household debt.
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] Private Credit Size: Growing but smaller than US/Europe.
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Massive insurance sector (Japan Post, etc.).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$6 trillion (TSE).
    - [LLM] Foreign Participation %: ~30% (High in free float).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$8 trillion (JGBs).
    - [LLM] Corporate Bond Yield Spreads: Tight due to low risk-free rates.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Tokyo Stock Exchange (JPX).
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): JASDEC, JSCC.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: High (legally), but works closely with MOF.
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: High (due to domestic funding and yen status).
- **Rule of Law:**
    - [LLM] Regulatory Efficiency: High (JFSA).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: G7, Quad, US-Japan Alliance.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): A+ / A1 / A.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: JGB (Japanese Government Bond).
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: TIBOR, TONAR.
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: BoJ Policy Board (8 times/year).
    - [LLM] Key Events: Shunto (Spring wage talks), Tankan Survey.
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/365.
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: Nikkei 225, TOPIX, 10Y JGB Yield.
