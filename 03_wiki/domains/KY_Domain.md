# DOMAIN: Cayman Islands (KY)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Status:** British Overseas Territory.
- **Fiscal:** No direct income, corporate, or capital gains tax.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] Global Hub: World's leading domicile for offshore hedge funds and private equity.
    - [LLM] Shadow Banking: Critical node for the US Treasury Basis Trade.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] Currency: KYD (Dollar) - fixed peg 1.20 USD/KYD.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Monetary Authority:** CIMA (Cayman Islands Monetary Authority).
- **Liquidity:** Heavily dependent on USD wholesale funding markets.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Financial Services: ~50-60% GDP.
    - [LLM] Tourism: Secondary driver.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] Massive nominal assets (~$1 trillion+) relative to local GDP.
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Dominant (Thousands of registered funds).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: CSX (Cayman Islands Stock Exchange).

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: UK Territory, FATF Grey list monitoring.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Role in Global System:** "Hidden" liquidity pool for global macro funds.

## IX. Data Governance (Quản trị dữ liệu)
- **Official Sources:** [LLM] CIMA, ESO (Economics and Statistics Office).
