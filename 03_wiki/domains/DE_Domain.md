# DOMAIN: Germany (DE)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$2.6 trillion
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~65% (Maastricht criteria target 60%).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Diversified, highly liquid benchmark.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-1% to -2% (Constitutional debt brake).
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Often in surplus or slight deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Low, but rising with higher ECB rates.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+6% to +8% (Chronic surplus).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Massive exporter of high-value goods (Autos, Machinery).
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Steady, significant outbound FDI.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Bunds are the euro-area safe haven.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$300 billion (part of Eurosystem).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): N/A (Part of EUR zone).
    - [LLM] External Debt: Net international investment position is strongly positive.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$3 trillion (Bundesbank contribution to ECB balance sheet).
    - [LLM] QE/QT Status: ECB Quantitative Tightening (QT) in progress.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: ECB Deposit Facility (~4.0% in 2024), Main Refi (~4.5%).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive/Neutral as Eurozone inflation cools.
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: ESTR (Euro Short-Term Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): ECB MRO (Main Refinancing Ops), LTRO.
