# DOMAIN: United Arab Emirates (AE)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$160 billion (Aggregate Federal + Emirates).
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~30%.
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Mix of AED local bonds and USD Global Bonds.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: Consistently in surplus (~5% - 8% depending on oil).
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Low.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: Massive surplus (~10% - 15%).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Large surplus, hub for re-exports.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Hub for regional investment and real estate.
    - [LLM] Sovereign Wealth Funds: ADIA, Mubadala, ICD (among world's largest).
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$170 billion (Official reserves, excluding SWFs).
    - [LLM] Currency: Pegged to USD (3.67 AED/USD).

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$180 billion.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Base Rate (Follows US Fed Funds).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive.
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: EIBOR (legacy), DONIA (Dirham Overnight Index Average).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): M-Bills, Repo.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Non-Oil Sector: ~70% (Trade, Tourism, Finance, Real Estate).
    - [LLM] Oil Sector: ~30%.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~60%.
    - [LLM] Industry: ~39% (mostly O&G and Aluminum).
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Workforce: ~90% Expatriates.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
