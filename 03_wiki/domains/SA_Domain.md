# DOMAIN: Saudi Arabia (SA)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$280 billion.
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~25% (Very low, but rising to fund Vision 2030).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Mix of SAR Sukuk and Global Bonds (USD).
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: Volatile (Oil dependent); breakeven oil price ~80-85 USD.
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Shifted to deficit in 2024 to support investment.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Low but rising (~5-7%).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: Surplus (~3-5%), highly sensitive to oil prices.
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Massive surplus (Oil exports).
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Focus of Vision 2030 (Targeting $100B/year).
    - [LLM] PIF: Public Investment Fund is the primary vehicle for global capital recycling.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$450 - $470 billion (managed by SAMA).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~18 months.
    - [LLM] Currency: Fixed peg to USD (3.75 SAR/USD).

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$500 billion (SAMA).
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Repo Rate (Follows US Fed Funds due to peg).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Highly positive.
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: SAIBOR (Saudi Interbank Offered Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): SAMA Repo and Reverse Repo.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Oil Sector: ~40% GDP (Declining share as per Vision 2030).
    - [LLM] Non-Oil Sector: ~60% (Growing at 4-5%).
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Mining & Quarrying: ~40% (Crude oil & Gas).
    - [LLM] Services: ~45% (Government, Finance, Trade).
    - [LLM] Construction: Rising share (Giga-projects like NEOM).
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~8-9% (Saudi nationals), falling.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~20% (Very strong).
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~1.5% (Low).
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~95% - 100% (Tightening due to mortgage boom).
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~60%.
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Growing Fintech and Finance companies.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$3 trillion (Tadawul, dominated by Saudi Aramco).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$150 billion.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Tadawul.
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): Muqassa (Clearing), Edaa (Depository).

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: Moderate (SAMA).
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: High (Supported by massive oil wealth).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: G20, OPEC+, GCC.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): A / A1 / A+.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: KSA Sukuk.
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: SAIBOR.
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: Follows US FOMC meetings.
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/360.
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: TASI (Tadawul All Share Index).
