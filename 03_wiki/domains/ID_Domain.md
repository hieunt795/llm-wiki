# DOMAIN: Indonesia (ID)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$500 billion.
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~38% - 40% (Conservative fiscal rules).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Domestic and Global Bonds (SUN, INDON).
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: Cap of -3% (strictly enforced).
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: ~15%, a key fiscal metric.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~-0.5% to +0.5% (Commodity sensitive).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Large surplus, driven by Coal, Nickel, Palm Oil.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Heavy in nickel processing and mining.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Large and volatile bond market flows.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$140 - $150 billion.
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~6 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: Managed but sensitive to JPY and USD rates.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$200 billion.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: BI 7-Day Reverse Repo Rate (~6.0% in 2024).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive (Inflation ~3% vs Rate 6%).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: INDONIA (Indonesia Overnight Index Average).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): SRBI (BI Securities), Term Repo.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~55% (Main driver).
    - [LLM] Investment: ~30%.
    - [LLM] Net Exports: Commodity-led contributor.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~45%.
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~20% (Nickel refining, FMCG).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~13%.
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~5.3% (Improving).
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: ~3% - 5% YoY.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
