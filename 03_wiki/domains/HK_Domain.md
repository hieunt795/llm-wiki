# DOMAIN: Hong Kong (HK)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Status:** SAR of China.
- **Fiscal:** Low tax regime, massive fiscal reserves (~$100 billion+).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] Gateway to China: Stock/Bond Connect programs.
    - [LLM] Offshore Yuan (CNH): Largest global hub.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$420 billion (Massive relative to population).
    - [LLM] Currency: HKD pegged to USD (7.75-7.85 range) via LERS.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Monetary Authority:** HKMA (Hong Kong Monetary Authority).
- **Policy:** Effectively follows US Fed to maintain the peg.
- **Money Market:** HIBOR (Hong Kong Interbank Offered Rate).

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~93% GDP (Trade, Finance, Logistics).
    - [LLM] Note: Highly dependent on re-export trade with China.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] One of the world's highest bank densities.
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~20%.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$4-5 trillion (HKEX).
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: HKEX (Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing).

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): AA+ / Aa3 / AA.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **LERS:** Linked Exchange Rate System is the foundation of HK financial stability.

## IX. Data Governance (Quản trị dữ liệu)
- **Official Sources:** [LLM] HKMA, C&SD (Census and Statistics Department).
- **Update Frequency:** High.
