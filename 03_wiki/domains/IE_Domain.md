# DOMAIN: Ireland (IE)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Fiscal:** Corporate tax hub for tech and pharma multinationals.
- **Sovereign Debt Profile:** Significant improvement, debt/GDP ~40% - 45% (GNI* based).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] MNC Hub: Significant intellectual property (IP) and FDI flows.
    - [LLM] Funds Center: World's leading hub for ETF and alternative fund administration.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank:** Central Bank of Ireland / ECB.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~75%.
    - [LLM] Industry: ~24% (Pharma, ICT).
- **Note:** GDP is often distorted by MNC accounting (use GNI* for local reality).

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Shadow Banking:** One of the world's largest NBFI sectors relative to GDP.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:** Euronext Dublin.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch):** AA / Aa3 / AA.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Context:** Primary European hub for US-based multinational tax and capital structures.

## IX. Data Governance (Quản trị dữ liệu)
- **Official Sources:** [LLM] Central Bank of Ireland, CSO (Central Statistics Office).
