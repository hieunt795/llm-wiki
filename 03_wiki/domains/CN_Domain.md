# DOMAIN: China (CN)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$15 trillion (estimated, including LGFV)
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~80% (Central + Local Gov), ~300% (Total debt including corporate).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Heavy reliance on local government bonds.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-3% to -4% (Official), ~-7% to -8% (Augmented).
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Rising burden on local governments.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+1% to +2% (Structural surplus).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Largest goods surplus globally.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Significant but slowing recently.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Strictly managed via Bond/Stock Connect.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$3.2 trillion (World's largest).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~18 months (Extremely high).
    - [LLM] External Debt: Relatively low as % of GDP, mostly corporate.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$6 trillion
    - [LLM] QE/QT Status: Managed liquidity via RRR and MLF.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: 7-day Reverse Repo (~1.8%), 1Y LPR (~3.45%).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive (Low inflation environment).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: SHIBOR (Shanghai Interbank Offered Rate), DR007.
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): MLF (Medium-term Lending Facility), PSL.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~38% (Low compared to G7).
    - [LLM] Investment: ~43% (Extremely high, property & infrastructure).
    - [LLM] Government Spending: ~15%
    - [LLM] Net Exports: Significant contributor.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~54%
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~39%
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~7%
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~5.0% (Urban), youth unemployment is a key concern.
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: ~4-5% YoY.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~15%
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~1.6% (Official), likely higher in property portfolios.
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~80%
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~280% (Systemic leverage is high).
    - [LLM] Corporate vs Household Leverage: Massive corporate debt (state-owned), household debt rising.
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] Private Credit Size: Significant (~$6-8 trillion range in various forms).
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Trusts, Wealth Management Products (WMP).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$10-12 trillion (SSE + SZSE).
    - [LLM] Foreign Participation %: ~5% (Managed via quotas).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$5 trillion (CGBs + Local Gov Bonds).
    - [LLM] Corporate Bond Yield Spreads: Credit differentiation between SOEs and POEs.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Shanghai (SSE), Shenzhen (SZSE), Beijing (BSE), HKEX (Connect).
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): CCDC, SHCH.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: Low (Under State Council).
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: High for central government, lower for municipalities.
- **Rule of Law:**
    - [LLM] Regulatory Efficiency: High but subject to sudden policy shifts (e.g., tech crackdowns).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: BRICS, SCO.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): A+ / A1 / A+.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: CGB (China Government Bond), LGB (Local Government Bond).
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: SHIBOR, DR007 (Seven-day Repo rate).
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: NPC/CPPCC "Two Sessions" (March), Politburo meetings.
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/365.
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: CSI 300, 10Y CGB Yield.
