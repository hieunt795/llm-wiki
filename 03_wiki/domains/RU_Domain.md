# DOMAIN: Russia (RU)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$350 billion
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~15% - 20% (One of the lowest globally).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Domestic OFZ bonds dominate.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-2% to -3% (War economy spending).
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Rising due to high CBR rates (~16%+).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+3% to +5% (Surplus driven by energy exports).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Large surplus, though imports are shifting to Asia.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Near zero from West, growing from "friendly" nations.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Strictly restricted by capital controls.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$600 billion (approx. half frozen by West, remainder in Gold/Yuan).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~10-12 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: Significant deleveraging since 2022.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$800 billion.
    - [LLM] QE/QT Status: Managed liquidity via Repo and FX auctions.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: ~16.0% (as of early 2024, to combat inflation).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Highly positive.
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: RUONIA.
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): Main Repo, Lombard list.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~50%
    - [LLM] Investment: ~20% (High state/military investment).
    - [LLM] Government Spending: ~25% (Rising).
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~60%
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~35% (Energy, Military-Industrial Complex).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~5%
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~3.0% (Record lows due to labor shortage).
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: High (~10%+ nominal) due to labor scarcity.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~12% - 13%
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~5% - 6% (Official).
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~90%
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~60%
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] Private Credit Size: Small.
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Limited.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$600 billion (MOEX).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$200 billion (OFZ).
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Moscow Exchange (MOEX).
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): NSD (National Settlement Depository), NCC.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: Technically high, but under war pressure.
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: High (due to low debt), but sanctioned.
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: BRICS+, SCO, EAEU.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): Withdrawn / Default/Restricted.
