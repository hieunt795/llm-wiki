# DOMAIN: United States (US)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$34 trillion (2024)
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~120%
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Weighted average maturity ~6 years
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-6% to -7%
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Rising sharply, approaching 15-20% of tax revenue.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~-3% (Structural deficit)
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Large deficit in goods, surplus in services.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Largest recipient globally.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Massive inflows into Treasuries and Equities.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$240 billion (mostly SDRs and gold, as USD is the reserve currency).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): N/A (Reserve currency status).
    - [LLM] External Debt: High nominal, but denominated in own currency.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$7.5 trillion (Post-QT)
    - [LLM] QE/QT Status: Quantitative Tightening (QT) active in 2024-2025.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Fed Funds Rate (5.25% - 5.50% range in 2024).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive (Fed Funds - PCE).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: SOFR (Secured Overnight Financing Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): ON RRP (Reverse Repo), SRF (Standing Repo Facility).

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~68% (Main driver)
    - [LLM] Investment: ~17%
    - [LLM] Government Spending: ~17%
    - [LLM] Net Exports: Negative contribution.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~80%
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~18%
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~1-2%
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~3.5% - 4.0% (Natural rate)
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: ~4% YoY (Post-pandemic trend).

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~15%
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~1.2% (Rising in commercial real estate)
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~70%
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~210%
    - [LLM] Corporate vs Household Leverage: High corporate debt, household debt stabilizing.
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] Private Credit Size: Massive (~$1.7 trillion market)
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Dominant (Mutual funds, Penssion funds).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$50 trillion (NYSE + NASDAQ)
    - [LLM] Foreign Participation %: ~15-20%
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$27 trillion (Marketable debt)
    - [LLM] Corporate Bond Yield Spreads: Measured via IG/HY spreads vs Treasuries.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: NYSE, NASDAQ, CME.
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): DTCC, OCC.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: High (despite political noise)
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: Moderate (due to political polarization and debt ceiling issues).
- **Rule of Law:**
    - [LLM] Regulatory Efficiency: High (SEC, FINRA).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: NATO, G7, Five Eyes.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): AA+ / Aaa / AA+.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: Treasuries (Bills, Notes, Bonds), TIPS.
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: SOFR (Replacement for LIBOR).
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: FOMC (8 times/year).
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/360 (Money Market), Actual/Actual (Bonds).
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: S&P 500, Dow 30, 10Y Treasury Yield.
