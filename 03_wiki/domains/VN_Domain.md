# DOMAIN: Vietnam (VN)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$160 billion
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~37% - 40% (Well below the 60% ceiling).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Domestic VGBs dominate, increasing average maturity (~12 years).
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-3.5% to -4.0%.
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Low but rising.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+3% to +5% (Driven by trade surplus).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Significant surplus, driven by FDI manufacturing exports.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: ~$20-25 billion/year (High consistency).
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Highly reactive to USD/VND volatility.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$85 - $100 billion (Fluctuating based on SBV intervention).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~3.0 - 3.5 months (IMF threshold boundary).
    - [LLM] External Debt: Significant, mostly corporate and development-linked.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$150 billion.
    - [LLM] QE/QT Status: Managed liquidity via OMO (T-bills and Reverse Repos).
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Refinancing Rate (4.5%), Discount Rate (3.0%) as of early 2024.
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Neutral/Positive depending on CPI pulse.
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: Vietnam Interbank Offered Rate (ONIBOR).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): SBV OMO window.
- **Transmission Frictions:**
    - [LLM] **Credit Quotas (Room tín dụng):** Biến số chặn hành chính quan trọng nhất. Ngay cả khi lãi suất Interbank thấp và thanh khoản dồi dào, việc hết "Room" khiến ngân hàng không thể giải ngân, duy trì lãi suất cho vay ở mức cao.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~65%
    - [LLM] Investment: ~33% (Heavy infrastructure and FDI).
    - [LLM] Government Spending: ~10%
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~42%
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~38% (High-tech, Textiles, Footwear).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~12%
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~2.3% (Officially low).
    - [LLM] Wage Growth: ~5% - 7% YoY.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~11% - 12% (Lower than regional peers).
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~2% (Official), ~4-5% (Including restructured loans).
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~80% - 100% (High).
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~146% (High for income level).
    - [LLM] Corporate vs Household Leverage: Massive real estate corporate debt.
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] Private Credit Size: Growing corporate bond market.
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Limited.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$250 billion (HOSE + HNX).
    - [LLM] Foreign Participation %: ~15-20%.
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$80 billion.
    - [LLM] Corporate Bond Yield Spreads: High volatility in real estate paper.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: HOSE (Ho Chi Minh), HNX (Hanoi).
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): VSDC (Vietnam Securities Depository).

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: Low (Under Government).
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: High (due to conservative debt levels).
- **Rule of Law:**
    - [LLM] Regulatory Efficiency: Improving, but subject to administrative intervention (e.g., credit quotas).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: ASEAN, CPTPP, EVFTA, Comprehensive Strategic Partnership with US/China/Japan.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): BB+ / Ba2 / BB+.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: VGB (Vietnam Government Bond).
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: VNIBOR.
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: No fixed schedule; ad-hoc based on Government/SBV.
    - [LLM] Key Features: Credit Quotas (Room tín dụng) assigned to banks annually.
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/365.
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: VN-Index, 10Y VGB Yield.

- **Market Data Providers:** [LLM] FiinGroup, SSI, VNDIRECT.
