# DOMAIN: Malaysia (MY)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$260 billion.
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~60% - 62%.
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Domestic MGS (Malaysian Government Securities) and GII (Islamic).
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-4% to -5% (Post-COVID consolidation).
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Significant (~14-16%).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+2% to +3% (Structural surplus).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Large surplus, driven by Electronics (Semiconductors) and O&G.
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Tech-focused (Data centers, Semiconductors).
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Deep and liquid local bond market attracts FPI.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$110 - $115 billion.
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~5 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: High nominal, but large portion is internal inter-company debt.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$130 billion.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Overnight Policy Rate (OPR) (~3.0% in 2024).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive (OPR 3% vs CPI ~2%).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: MYOR (Malaysia Overnight Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): BNM Monetary Notes.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~60%.
    - [LLM] Net Exports: Major O&G and Electronics exporter.
    - [LLM] Investment: ~20%.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~58%.
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~24% (Semiconductor hub).
    - [LLM] Mining & Quarrying: ~6% (PETRONAS).
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~3.3% - 3.5% (Full employment).

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~18% (Strong).
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~1.7% (Low).
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~88%.
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~130%.
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] Private Credit Size: Growing Islamic finance sector.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$350 - $400 billion (Bursa Malaysia).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$200 billion.
    - [LLM] Sukuk Market: Global leader in Islamic bonds.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Bursa Malaysia.
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): BM Clearing, BM Depository.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)

- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: ASEAN, CPTPP, IPEF.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): A- / A3 / BBB+.
