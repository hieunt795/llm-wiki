# DOMAIN: Luxembourg (LU)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Fiscal:** Highly favorable corporate tax environment.
- **Sovereign Debt:** AAA rated, extremely low debt/GDP (~25%).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] UCITS Hub: Europe's leading center for investment funds (~€5 trillion AUM).
    - [LLM] Cross-border Flow: Primary gateway for EU capital distribution.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank:** BCL (Banque centrale du Luxembourg) / ECB.
- **Money Market:** Critical node in the Euro interbank market.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Financial Sector: ~25-30% GDP directly.
    - [LLM] Steel: ArcelorMittal HQ.

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] Over 120 international banks present.

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Luxembourg Stock Exchange (LuxSE) - world leader in green bonds.
