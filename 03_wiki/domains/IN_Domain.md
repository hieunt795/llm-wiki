# DOMAIN: India (IN)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$2.5 trillion.
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~80% - 82% (High for EM).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Long average maturity, mostly domestic-owned G-Secs.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-5% to -6%.
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Significant (~25-30% of central revenue).

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~-1% to -2% (Structural deficit, commodity sensitive).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Deficit in goods (Oil, Gold), surplus in services (IT).
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Heavy in Services and Manufacturing (PLI scheme).
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Large equity inflows; upcoming inclusion in JPM GBI-EM bond index.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$620 - $640 billion (Top 5 globally).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~10-11 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: Managed, significant portion is long-term.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$850 billion.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: Repo Rate (~6.5% in 2024).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive (Repo 6.5% vs CPI ~5%).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: WARE (Weighted Average Repo Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): SDF (Standing Deposit Facility), MSF.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~60% (Main driver).
    - [LLM] Investment: ~30% (Rising via GVC shifts).
    - [LLM] Government Spending: ~10%.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~54% (IT, Finance).
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~28% (Manufacturing share ~15%).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~18% (Large labor share).
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~7% - 8% (Youth unemployment concerns).

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~16% - 17% (Multi-year high).
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~3.0% - 3.5% (Multi-year low).
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~75%.
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~55% (Deepening stage).
- **Shadow Banking:**
    - [LLM] NBFI Presence: Large NBFC sector (HDFC, Bajaj Finance).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$4.5 trillion (NSE + BSE).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$1.2 trillion (G-Sec).
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: NSE (National), BSE (Bombay).
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): NCL, ICCL.

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: High (RBI).
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: Improving (Consolidation path).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: Quad, BRICS+, G20.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): BBB- (Stable/Positive).

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: G-Sec (Government Securities), SDL (State Development Loans).
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: MIBOR (Mumbai Interbank Offered Rate).
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: MPC (6 times/year).
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: 30/360.
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: Nifty 50, Sensex.

## IX. Data Governance (Quản trị dữ liệu)
- **Official Sources:** [LLM] RBI, MOSPI (Statistics Ministry), Ministry of Finance.
- **Market Data Providers:** [LLM] Bloomberg, Reuters, NSE.
