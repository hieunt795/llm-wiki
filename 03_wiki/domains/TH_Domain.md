# DOMAIN: Thailand (TH)

## I. Sovereign & Fiscal (Quốc gia & Tài khóa)
- **Sovereign Debt Profile (Stock):**
    - [LLM] Total Public Debt: ~$320 billion.
    - [LLM] Debt/GDP Ratio: ~62% (Post-COVID rising).
    - [LLM] Maturity Structure: Domestic LB (Loan Bonds) dominate.
- **Fiscal Balance (Flow):**
    - [LLM] Budget Balance/GDP: ~-3% to -4%.
    - [LLM] Primary Balance: Deficit.
- **Interest Burden:**
    - [LLM] Interest Expense/Revenue: Moderate.

## II. External Sector (Khu vực đối ngoại)
- **Balance of Payments:**
    - [LLM] Current Account/GDP: ~+1% to +3% (Tourism dependent).
    - [LLM] Trade Balance: Surplus in manufacturing (Autos, Electronics).
- **Capital Flows:**
    - [LLM] FDI Inflow: Competition from Vietnam/Indonesia.
    - [LLM] Portfolio Flow (FPI): Large bond and equity flows.
- **Vulnerability:**
    - [LLM] FX Reserves: ~$210 - $230 billion (Very high).
    - [LLM] Import Cover (Months): ~9-10 months.
    - [LLM] External Debt: Managed, mostly private.

## III. Monetary & Liquidity (Tiền tệ & Thanh khoản)
- **Central Bank Balance Sheet:**
    - [LLM] Asset Size: ~$280 billion.
- **Policy Rates:**
    - [LLM] Key Policy Rate: 1-Day Repo Rate (~2.5% in 2024).
    - [LLM] Real Interest Rate: Positive (Low inflation environment).
- **Money Market:**
    - [LLM] Benchmark O/N Rate: THOR (Thai Overnight Repurchase Rate).
    - [LLM] Liquidity Facilities (OMO/Repo): BoT Bill auctions.

## IV. Real Economy Structure (Kinh tế thực)
- **GDP Breakdown:**
    - [LLM] Consumption: ~50%.
    - [LLM] Net Exports: ~10% contribution.
    - [LLM] Investment: ~25%.
- **Sectoral Contribution:**
    - [LLM] Services: ~60% (Tourism is ~12-15% GDP).
    - [LLM] Industry/Manufacturing: ~30% (Detroit of the East).
    - [LLM] Agriculture: ~8%.
- **Labor Market:**
    - [LLM] Unemployment Rate: ~1.0% (Structurally very low).

## V. Financial System (Hệ thống tài chính)
- **Banking Sector:**
    - [LLM] System-wide CAR: ~19% (Robust).
    - [LLM] NPL Ratio: ~2.7% (Official), concerns over SME/Household debt.
    - [LLM] Loan-to-Deposit Ratio (LDR): ~90%.
- **Credit Growth:**
    - [LLM] Total Credit/GDP: ~160%.
    - [LLM] Household Debt/GDP: ~90% (Among the highest in Asia).

## VI. Capital Markets & Infrastructure (Thị trường vốn)
- **Equity Market:**
    - [LLM] Market Cap: ~$500 billion (SET).
- **Debt Market:**
    - [LLM] Gov Bond Market Size: ~$200 billion.
- **Market Infrastructure:**
    - [LLM] Key Exchanges: Stock Exchange of Thailand (SET), TFEX.
    - [LLM] Clearing & Settlement (FMI): TSD (Thailand Securities Depository).

## VII. Institutional & Risk Overlay (Thể chế & Rủi ro)
- **Institutional Quality:**
    - [LLM] Central Bank Independence: High (Bank of Thailand).
    - [LLM] Fiscal Credibility: Good (Public Debt Management Act).
- **Geopolitics:**
    - [LLM] Major Alliances: ASEAN, US Treaty Ally.
    - [LLM] Risk Rating (S&P/Moody's/Fitch): BBB+ / Baa1 / BBB+.

## VIII. Local Market Intelligence (Tình báo bản địa)
- **Market Naming:**
    - [LLM] Sovereign Bond Names: LB (Loan Bond), T-Bill.
    - [LLM] Local Interbank Rates: BIBOR (legacy) -> THOR.
- **Policy Cycles:**
    - [LLM] Major Policy Meeting Cycle: MPC (6 times/year).
- **Trading Conventions:**
    - [LLM] Day Count Convention: Actual/365.
    - [LLM] Local Benchmarks: SET Index.
