## Extracted Official Data

[RAW-OFFICIAL:MOF Securities Flows] The weekly release covers International Transactions in Securities based on reports from designated major investors, and MOF defines positive net figures as net acquisition and negative net figures as net disposition.

[RAW-OFFICIAL:MOF Securities Flows] For portfolio investment liabilities, meaning nonresident transactions in Japanese securities, the week of June 21-27, 2026 recorded net disposition of JPY 1,816.5 billion in equity and investment fund shares.

[RAW-OFFICIAL:MOF Securities Flows] For the same week, nonresidents recorded net disposition of JPY 493.7 billion in long-term debt securities and JPY 2,426.1 billion in short-term debt securities.

[RAW-OFFICIAL:MOF Securities Flows] The same week's total nonresident net disposition across Japanese securities was JPY 4,736.3 billion.

[RAW-OFFICIAL:MOF Securities Flows] The monthly May 2026 release recorded nonresident net acquisition of JPY 3,105.8 billion across Japanese securities, consisting of JPY 2,927.2 billion in equity and investment fund shares, JPY 894.5 billion in long-term debt securities, and net disposition of JPY 716.0 billion in short-term debt securities.

## Working Interpretation

[LLM-S] The June 21-27 weekly outflow is therefore a late-quarter de-risking signal, not proof that foreign investors were net sellers of Japanese securities throughout H1 2026.
