## Curve Structure Analysis

| Spread | Value | Interpretation |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| 2s10s | **+31bp** | Positive — thoát inversion |
| 3m10y | **+56bp** | Positive — steepening |
| 2s30s | **+77bp** | Moderate steepening |
| 10s30s | **+46bp** | Long-end relatively flat |
| 3m30y | **+102bp** | Full curve slope |

**Curve regime:** Bear steepening — driven by term premium recovery + fiscal supply, NOT easing expectations.
- Short end (3M–2Y) is anchored by EFFR 3.63%, market pricing moderate cuts
- Long end (10Y–30Y) elevated by term premium (ACM +0.73–0.83%, see separate file)
- 20Y > 30Y anomaly: 20Y liquidity premium (20Y auction smaller/less liquid than 30Y)
